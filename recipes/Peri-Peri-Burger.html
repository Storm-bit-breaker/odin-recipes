Looking for something with a fiery kick? Why not try our odin's peri-peri chicken burger

ingredients

  • Brioche Bun
  • Red Chilli
  • Malt Vinegar
  • 2 slices of cheese (American cheddar cheese)
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Onion
  • Paprika
  • Oregano
  • Sour Cream
  • Mayonnaise
  • chicken Fillets

steps

  1. Put chicken fillets into a freezer bag. Then using mallet or rolling pin to reduce the thickness of fillets slightly
  2. Cut the fillets sizes slightly larger than bun sizes. Factor in 20% shrinkage
  3. Blend all the ingredients for the sauce. Mix 3 table spooons with the sour cream and mayonnaise to make the Peri-naise sauce
  4. Use 1/2 cup of the sauce to marinade the chicken. Leave to marinade at least 3 hours up to 24 hours
  5. Cook the chicken on the stove or BBQ using medium heat. Avoid using high heat so marinade won't get burnt
  6. Once cooked leave the chicken to rest for 3-5 minutes before assemmbling the burger
  7. Toast the brioche buns on the skillet until golden brown (Optional).
  8. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, cheerse and odins special peri-naise sauce.
