Smash Burger

Home

Welcome to the Odin House, home of the famous odin smash burger.

This is "loki" one of the best smash burgers around!

ingredients

  • Brioche Bun
  • 1 pound of 80% Ground Beef
  • Salt and Pepper Season
  • 2 slices of cheese (American cheddar cheese)
  • Lettuce, tomato, avacado, onions
  • Special secret odin sauce

steps

  1. Preheat a cast iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 2 equal portions and shape them into balls.
  3. Make the odin special sauce using ketchup,mayonnaise,mustard,garlic, and some hot mild hot sauce.
  4. Place the balls on the skillet and smash them down with a spatula to form patties.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then cook for about 2-3 minutes until browned.
  6. Flip the patties, add cheese, and cook for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese melts.
  7. Toast the brioche buns on the skillet until golden brown (Melt Butter can to add some flavour to the buns).
  8. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, avocado, onions, and odins special sauce.
